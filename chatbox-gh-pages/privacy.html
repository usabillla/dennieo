If you contact us by e-mail, using a contact form on the Services, or by mail or other means, we collect the personal information contained within, and associated with, your correspondence.

When you use our Services, some information is collected automatically. For example, we may automatically collect your browser’s Internet Protocol (IP) address, your browser type, the nature of the device from which you using the Services, the identifier for any handheld or mobile device that you may be using, the actions you take while using the Services, and the content, features, and activities that you access and participate in while using the Services. 2. Security

We take appropriate precautions to protect our users' information. Your information is located on secure servers and encrypted before it hits our disks. We use https to protect information in transit when you use the Chatbox web application. This applies to your personal information and chat messages.

Please note that Chatbox may send an email digest containing chat messages to some room participants, depending on a user’s settings. These digests are not encrypted and are only as secure as any other email.

Links sent to you in “Invitation emails”, “Digest emails” and “Mention emails” are effectively your gateway into Chatbox. If you forward, share, or otherwise divulge those links to a third party, your Chatbox account may be compromised and you may be impersonated.

3. Storage, Disclosure and Use of Your Communications

By using Chatbox for chatting to just one person, you consent to the storage of your chat history on Chatbox application as the default option. By using Chatbox to chat in rooms with other users, your chat history is saved as the default option. For more information about IM history features and settings, please see Chatbox's support documentation.

4. Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer's hard drive by our servers so our servers can identify your computer. We use cookies to record session information. We will not share any personally identifiable information stored in the Chatbox cookies with outside companies for any purpose without your permission.

5. Data Storage

When you use the file transfer feature of Chatbox, the file that you transfer is copied and retained on the servers of our third party service provider after the transfer. Tetrapod does not access or uses these files for any purpose, but please note that these files are not encrypted and could be disclosed in legal proceedings or investigations.

6. Children

Children’s safety is important to us, and we encourage parents and guardians to take an active interest in the online activities of their children. Our Services are not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 without obtaining parental consent. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under the age of 13 on our Services, we will delete that information as quickly as possible. If you believe that we may have collected any such personal information on our Services, please notify us at legal@chatbox.com..
